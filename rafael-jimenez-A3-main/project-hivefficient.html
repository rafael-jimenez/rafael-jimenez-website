Hivefficient – Client Website

(Project completed May 2025)

Tap the image to view their LinkedIn page

Project Overview

Hivefficient is a virtualization and IT services company. I started a full redesign of their homepage to modernize the layout, update branding, and create a cleaner user experience.

Development Challenges

The project was short and ended early, but it taught me how real client work can change direction unexpectedly. I gained experience adapting my workflow and adjusting designs based on evolving needs.

Reflection & Learning

Even though it was brief, I learned how to communicate with clients, collect requirements, and approach a redesign with usability in mind. It strengthened my UI design skills and gave me real-world experience in project adaptability.
